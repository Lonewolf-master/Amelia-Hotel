# Product Guidelines

## Content & Prose Style
The website's content should strike a balance between professional luxury and a warm, welcoming atmosphere. It should be:
- **Professional and Trustworthy:** Focused on reliability and high-end service.
- **Warm and Welcoming:** Friendly and inviting, making guests feel at home.
- **Modern and Minimalist:** Concise and sophisticated for the contemporary traveler.

## Visual Identity & Design Principles
The visual design will reflect a **Classic Luxury** aesthetic while incorporating modern interactive elements:
- **Typography:** Elegant serif fonts for headings and clear, readable fonts for body text.
- **Color Palette:** A sophisticated palette including gold, navy, and deep neutral tones.
- **Imagery & Media:** 
    - High-contrast, high-resolution photography of luxury hotel interiors and exteriors.
    - Integration of high-quality background or feature videos to showcase the atmosphere.
- **Interactivity:** Use of **GSAP (GreenSock Animation Platform)** for smooth, premium transitions and scroll-triggered animations.
- **Responsiveness:** A "mobile-first" approach ensuring the luxury experience is seamless across all screen sizes (desktop, tablet, mobile).

## Brand Messaging & Core Features
- **Exclusive Escape:** Emphasize privacy, personalized service, and a retreat from the everyday.
- **Interactive Location:** Clearly display the hotel's location with a prominent "Find Our Location" button. Upon clicking, it should reveal an embedded Google Map showing the route from the user's current position.
