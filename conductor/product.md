# Initial Concept
Apart Hotel Amelia: A modern luxury website with smooth GSAP animations, featuring a full booking system, room management, and a high-end UI (Deep Green/Navy, Gold accents).

# Product Definition: Apart Hotel Amelia

## Vision
Apart Hotel Amelia is a modern luxury hospitality platform designed to blend high-end sleekness with warm, welcoming service. The website serves as a sophisticated digital storefront that prioritizes visual storytelling through advanced GSAP animations, ensuring a bold and memorable first impression.

## Target Audience
- **Luxury Travelers:** Individuals seeking premium amenities, elegant design, and superior service.
- **Business Professionals:** Travelers looking for professional, convenient, and high-quality apartment-style accommodations.
- **Tourists & Families:** Visitors exploring Buea and its surroundings who value comfort and local charm.

## Core Features & Priorities
- **Interactive Visual Storytelling:** Leveraging GSAP for an immersive hero section (parallax, text reveals), fluid room showcases, and scroll-triggered content reveals.
- **Interactive Discovery:** A core experience featuring a GSAP-animated hero section, interactive room gallery with scroll-triggered animations, and integrated location discovery.
- **Comprehensive Admin Dashboard:** Tools for hotel staff to manage room availability, update pricing, and analyze occupancy rates.
- **Omnichannel Engagement:** Seamless direct communication through a floating WhatsApp concierge (+237 652422909) and elegant, validated contact forms (chidaluwisdomorima@gmail.com) for personalized inquiries.

## Brand Personality & Aesthetic
- **Sleek & Modern:** Minimalist precision paired with high-tech features.
- **Warm Luxury:** A hospitable and elegant atmosphere that feels like a refined "home away from home."
- **Bold Trendsetter:** Experimental layouts and unique animations that position the hotel as a leader in digital hospitality.
